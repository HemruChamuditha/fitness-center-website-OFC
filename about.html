About Us

Sri Lanka's very first medically focused gym

Sri Lanka's very first medically focused gym. Our wellness section contains a full-fledged medical clinic with doctors, nutritionists, sports medicine specialists, and other specialists on staff to guarantee that our members receive the finest care possible. It was designed by an award winning architect and is Sri Lanka's largest and most technologically sophisticated fitness center. As a result, this structure is not only the largest, but also the pinnacle of premium fitness and wellness centers in Sri Lanka!

We provide a wide range of premium services to our members.We have partnered with sri Lanka Hospital Diagnostics to provide a lab collection center onsite. kamal Fernando and nimal also having outlets at OZONE, where members enjoy special discounts for all their services! We also plan to collaborate with leading business partners in the near future to provide you the services of a fresh juice bar, a health food corner, a coffee shop, a fine dining restaurant, and a spa all under one roof.

Our only goal is to provide a remarkable service that is worth every penny you pay for!
